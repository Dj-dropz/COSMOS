Origin

        
Space is a vast expanse that houses an array of celestial bodies, including stars, planets, moons, and galaxies.

        
This project explores the wonders of the final frontier and provides insights into its deep mysteries.

    


    

        
Planets

        
From the iron core of Mercury to the frozen winds of Neptune, our solar system contains eight unique worlds orbiting a single star.

    


    

        
Stars

        
Massive spheres of plasma held together by their own gravity. Our Sun is just one of billions in the Milky Way alone.

    


    

        
Galaxies

        
Giant collections of stars, dust, and dark matter swirling in the void. There are estimated to be over 2 trillion galaxies in the observable universe.
